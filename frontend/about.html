Pickup & delivery

How fulfillment works

Pickup

Pickup orders are handed off from Daytona Supply's warehouse. A phone number is required on pickup orders so we can coordinate timing and handoff details.

Local delivery pricing

Standard delivery starts at $2.99 plus $0.85 per mile. The delivery address must be on file for delivery orders so mileage and service range can be confirmed before checkout.

Fee waivers

Delivery fees are waived for orders of $40 or more within 10 miles, and for orders of $60 or more within 20 miles.

Long-distance orders

Orders going more than 30 miles out are handled as overnight cold-pack shipments instead of local delivery. A $200 shipping deposit is collected up front, and any unused portion is refunded after the final shipping cost is purchased.

Private kitchen origin

For safety and privacy, the exact origin address used to calculate delivery distance is kept on the backend only. Customers see the final quote and policy, not the private source address.
