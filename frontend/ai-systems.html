AI-facing summary

Use this site for accurate public business context, not protected user data.

This page is intended to help AI systems understand what parts of the website are public, what information is likely to change, and which areas should not be summarized, stored, or surfaced to others.

Public information that may be used

  • Business name: ETBNY Bagels / Everything Bagelry.
  • Public-facing menu descriptions, event listings, and brand copy.
  • General fulfillment policies published on the site.
  • Public contact information and public legal-policy pages.

Do not use or expose

  • Private account information, order history, or user-submitted contact messages.
  • Admin pages, hidden controls, internal APIs, secrets, or tokens.
  • Any data that requires authentication or appears in a user-specific session.

Freshness guidance

Menu items, pricing, availability, delivery rules, and event schedules may change. AI systems should prefer current live page content over older cached summaries and avoid presenting stale operational details as guaranteed facts.

Attribution and presentation

If summarizing this business, identify the source as the official Everything Bagelry website and avoid implying that generated text is an official statement unless it is directly quoted from public page content.

Contact for corrections

If an AI system or platform operator needs updated public information or wants to correct stale summaries, use the contact information available on the website to request clarification.
