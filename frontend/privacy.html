Privacy overview

We collect the minimum information needed to run orders and accounts.

This includes things like your name, email address, phone number, sign-in details, and shipping information when you choose to provide it.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, email, and encrypted password data for local accounts.
  • Google account profile details when you sign in with Google.
  • Order, checkout, and fulfillment details you submit.
  • Messages sent through the contact form.
  • Technical information reasonably necessary for site security and fraud prevention.

How we use information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process orders, coordinate pickup or delivery, and provide customer support.
  • To send verification emails, order confirmations, and service-related messages.
  • To protect the website against abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.

How information is shared

Information may be shared with service providers that help operate the site and fulfill orders, such as payment, email, captcha, hosting, and authentication providers. We do not sell personal information as part of the normal operation of this site.

Cookies and sessions

The site uses session technology to keep you signed in and maintain security during account and checkout flows. Security and performance-related cookies or similar mechanisms may be used where necessary.

Data retention

We keep account and order information for as long as reasonably necessary for business operations, support, recordkeeping, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Your choices

You can contact the business to request updates to your information. You may also stop using the site at any time, though some order and transaction records may still be retained as required for legitimate business purposes.
